What is UMSI CareerLink?

UMSI CareerLink offers on-demand access to career support: internships, jobs, career coaching, career-building resources, special events and more. This online tool, which replaces the iTrack platform, is exclusively for enrolled UMSI students and alumni.

Some of the most important features to know on CareerLink to help you search for job/internship postings include:

  • OCI and Job Listings Tab - This is the tab where you'll go to search for opportunities in CareerLink. OCI stands for on-campus interviews, and job listings are postings of opportunities that you can apply for.
  • Filters - We recommend using filters within OCI and job listings to streamline your search as much as possible.
    • One of our favorite filters is the "Job Function" filter, which can be found by clicking "Add Filter" and then clicking "Job Function." The Job Function filter allows you to search by the type of work you want to do (i.e. UX Design, Library Science, Data Analytics, and/or Consulting).
  • "Turn on email alerts for this search" - You'll see this note in the bottom left corner of the OCI and Job Listings page. Click on it to set up automatic email alerts for when new opportunities that align with your filters are posted.
